Simple Philosophy

At Simple we have always been about being kind. When it comes to kindness, we believe that small acts are
just as impactful as grand gestures. Since our inception, we provide simple solutions in a world filled with complexity.
And that’s how we’ve gone to become U.K’s no.1 facial care brand.

who we are
Kindness is at the heart of everything we do – but – we’re about so
much more. With the aim of creating skincare that is fuss-free and
approachable, we’re committed to keeping you and your skin
looking (and feeling!) its best, starting with clean, uncomplicated
products that downright love your skin and the planet too.
Our journey started way back in the 1960’s, in the hands of a British
doctor, with the goal of offering kindness to sensitive skins. With 1978
marking the launch of our first ever no colour, no perfume and no
harsh chemicals soap, our attitude towards skincare is still the same!
Meaning your skin will love us, always.
we are free from
Our free-from list boasts more than 2000 harsh chemicals that are
harmful to your skin! But we don’t stop there, we are committed to
clean beauty and for that, every year 10% of our profits go towards
R&D. These efforts are to further work on our free-from list and
increase the number of blacklisted ingredients. Oh yes, we take a lot
of pride in this ever-increasing list!

Clean Beauty Is Simple!
We think looking after your skin should be simple, clean and free from
any harsh ingredients that could upset it. Which is why we only use
the kindest ingredients that respect your skin’s natural ways.
The world of skincare is always evolving, and we’re always learning.
Our goal is to make sure we keep up-to-date on innovations, our
free-from list (which already contains more than 2000 chemicals!)
